## About

OQTS is a quantitative trading society at the University of Oxford, founded in 2026 and run by students. We bring together people who want to explore and pursue quantitative finance, give them the tools and experience to do it seriously, and connect that talent with the firms that hire it.

Members will get hands-on experience writing trading algorithms on our own exchange, conducting collaborative research, and learning directly from practitioners through sponsor-led seminars. The result is a community of students with real, demonstrable quant experience — and a pre-screened pipeline for our partners.

## What we provide

- **OXDAQ — our live trading exchange.** A real-time, low-latency market simulator where members write Python algorithms and compete in live trading rounds. Built in-house; the matching engine and client SDK are open source (see [`oxdaq-core`](https://github.com/oqts/oxdaq-core)).
- **Seminars from leading firms.** Tech talks, lectures, and workshops delivered by practitioners from our sponsors — the sessions that show how quant trading and research actually work.
- **A Strategy & Research team.** An application-gated group conducting collaborative research and member-led projects across quantitative strategy.
- **A student-run quantitative fund.** Oxford's first student-run quant fund, launching late 2026 — paper-traded first, with live strategies to follow.
- **Member data & compute infrastructure.** *(Upcoming)* A unified, programmatic data-access API and shared cloud compute, funded through sponsorship — professional-grade historical data and resources available to members as part of the society.
- **Direct connections to industry.** Recruitment access, sponsor events, and engagement with the firms shaping the field.

## Membership

| Tier | Who | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Open** | All Oxford students | Seminars, talks, introductory content, and community |
| **Strategy Team** | By application — a rigorous assessment & interview process that mirrors real quant recruiting | Research, projects, and the OXDAQ exchange |
| **Core** | Founding & leadership team | Builds and runs the society's infrastructure |

The Strategy Team's selection process is deliberately demanding — it's how we ensure our members, and the talent we surface to recruiters, are genuinely strong.

## For recruiters & partners

OQTS gives partner firms access to a **pre-screened pool of candidates** who have:

- written and run live algorithms on a real exchange,
- undergone collaborative quantitative research, and
- attended technical seminars from leading practitioners.

Sponsorship includes speaker slots, recruitment access, and engagement with our Strategy Team. To partner with us, get in touch at **[oqts@oqts.org](mailto:oqts@oqts.org)**.
